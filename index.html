AI Feature #1

AI Pre-Moderates Every Review (Before Humans See It)

How it works

  • Customer submits review → Azure OpenAI gpt-5.4-mini analyzes → Auto-approve or flag

  • SHA-256 blacklist cache (90-day TTL)

    Prevents resubmission of rejected content

  • Rejection criteria: Profanity, spam, fake reviews, personal info, threats

  • Structured JSON output

    approved, reason, flags, confidence (0-1)

Benefits

  • 95%+ reviews auto-approved in <2 seconds

  • Manual review queue only for edge cases

  • Content deduplication prevents repeat spam

AI Feature #2

AI Generates Smart Product Suggestions

Similar Products

  • Based on category + manufacturer + price (±30%) + tags

  • Test result: 3 results with scores 0.6-0.7

  • Reasons: SAME_CATEGORY, SAME_MANUFACTURER

Complementary Products

  • AI (gpt-5.4) dynamically generates compatible categories

  • Example: "hydraulic pump" → AI suggests ["seals", "lubricants", "hoses", "filters"]

  • Test result: 3 results with AI scores up to 1.0

  • No hardcoded rules — AI learns product relationships
